Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our vehicles and services

Purchasing

You can start by browsing our online inventory or visiting our showroom. Our sales team will guide you through the selection process, arrange test drives, and help you find the perfect vehicle that matches your preferences and requirements.

Yes, we accept trade-ins and offer competitive valuations. Our experts will assess your vehicle and provide a fair market value that can be applied to your new purchase.

Financing

We offer various financing options including traditional loans, lease programs, and special financing for qualified buyers. Our finance team works with multiple lenders to find the best rates and terms for your situation.

While a higher credit score typically results in better rates, we work with customers across the credit spectrum. Contact our finance department to discuss your specific situation.

Warranty & Service

All our new vehicles come with manufacturer warranty coverage. We also offer extended warranty options and maintenance packages for additional peace of mind.

Service intervals vary by model, but typically we recommend maintenance every 5,000-7,500 miles. Our service department can provide a detailed maintenance schedule for your specific vehicle.
